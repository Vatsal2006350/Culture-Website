Top 5 Paintings in India you need to know about

  • Madhubani Painting:Madhubani Paintings are originated in the region of Bihar and are a form of wall art. The designs are made by geometrical patterns, scenes from the ancient myths, and symbolic images.

  • Phad Painting: This paintings style has originated in the state of Rajasthan, India. It is a scroll painitng style usually made on a long peice of canvas or cloth. They are also used as portable temples where the preists perform venerations in their presence.

  • Warli Painting: Warli paintings are 2500-year-old traditions, in the areas of Maharashtra. They showcase daily activities of the local people of the community like hunting, praying, farming, and dancing.

  • Kalamkari Painting: Kalamkari are 3000-year-old organic art, where hand and block painting was traditionally used for making art. Its name comes from “kalam” which is pen and originated in Andhra Pradesh. It involves earthy colors like green, rust, black and mustard.
