Should cultural diversity be promoted?

Type of diversity Advantages Disadvantages
Learning about other cultures Gives people an exposure of the traditions and cultures of another country, and their way of living. Allows people to think in a diverse way, and not be confined toward only thinking in one particular direciton. When people start to gain knowledge about other cultures, they become so influenced that they start to forget their own culture. This gives birth to cultural loss, and ruines the uniqueness between each individual.
Products made from different cultures Allows for a diverse range of products to be made. This way, it aids businesses in generating more profit as they are able to use the designs from different cultures to create unique and more fascinating products. Disadvantages are that not everyone may like the designs from other cultures. Some cultures may receive positive feedback and good publicity, however, some cultures may be critisized for the type of products they sell, and this can lead to racial discrimination, and hurts feelings of individuals from other cultures.    
Religous Allows an exchange of knowledge between different groups, so that they can learn more about other religions and the importance of different festivals in their country. Responsible citizens can effectively express their religions and ideas through exhibitions. A great example being the EXPO 2020 Dubai.        When people from different cultures live in one country, there is higher chances of religous conflicts. People may use religion as a mean of causing fights and riots, and an excuse for whatver wrong deeds they do.
